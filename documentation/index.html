Installation

Follow the following steps to upload our script to your server

Upload the app.zip to your server and extract it where you want to install SmartPost

After successful upload of the script , go to the installation screen by going to http://yourdomain.com/install

Follow the install screen and fill out the requested details to finish the installation

Provide your purchase code

Enter your database details

Also enter your admin accounts and make you take note of your password and email address
